About Aim Trainer

This is a simple aim training game to practice clicking accuracy and speed. Choose a game mode and difficulty, then press Start to begin.

Game Modes

  • Flick: Click the Robots as they respawn. Target is 10 successful hits.
  • Pulse: Robots spawn over time. Click them before too many appear. If 5 Robots are present at once you lose. Difficulty controls spawn rate.
  • Trio: Three Robots are present at a time. Click a target number of Robots to finish the round.
